How it works

Listing on Renters.com is always free. But if you want us to do the work of finding, screening, and delivering the right renter — that's our matching service, and it's worth it.

✓ Verified identity — real person, confirmed
✓ Known income and credit range — no surprises
✓ Rental history and known obstacles — upfront, not discovered later
✓ Their story in their own words — who they are and why they want your property
✓ Showing coordination — we help schedule and confirm so no one wastes time
✓ You only pay if a matched renter signs a lease — 50% of first month's rent
