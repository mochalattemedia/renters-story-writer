Step 3 of 5

Complete your listing

Every empty field is a question a renter has to ask before they can say yes. Fill everything out — no exceptions.

✓ Monthly rent — exact amount, no "contact for price"
✓ Availability date — when can someone move in?
✓ Lease terms — month-to-month, 6 months, 1 year?
✓ Pets, parking, utilities — what's included or allowed?
✓ A real description — tell the story of the space
✓ Number of bedrooms, bathrooms, and square footage
